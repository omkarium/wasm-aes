AES Encryption in your browser

by Omkaram



What is this Tool and How does it work?

This is a web based data encryption tool made available in WASM (WebAssembly). This helps you to do data encryption using AES256 in two modes. The App runs directly in your browser and does not transmit your key or data to anywhere else. If you are not convinced, then try loading the page just once and disconnect your machine to the internet. The app must be able to work regardless.
